Feel it first
You are your own witness.
This feeling belongs to me.
It is not an emergency.
I do not need to involve anyone else to make it real.
01
What just happened?
Facts only. No interpretation yet.
02
What am I actually feeling?
Go deeper than "hurt" or "mad." More than one can be true.
03
Is this about now — or is this familiar?
Big feelings about small things usually mean the small thing touched something older.
04
What does this feeling need from me?
From you, not from the other person.
05
Does anything actually need to be said to someone?
Most of the time the answer is "not right now" or "no."
