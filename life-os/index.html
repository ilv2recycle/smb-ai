The 5-step Generator protocol
1
Write the decision in one clear sentence below. No pros and cons yet — just state it.
2
Read it out loud. Notice your body first — expansion or contraction? That answer comes before the mental one.
3
Ask: "Is this a response to something that came toward me, or am I initiating from anxiety or pressure?" Generators drain when they force.
4
Moon-Saturn check: "Am I saying no because I'm scared of needing someone, or because it's a genuine no?" Fear of dependence is not discernment.
5
North Node check: "What would the more independent, self-trusting version of me do here?" That version is who you are becoming.
