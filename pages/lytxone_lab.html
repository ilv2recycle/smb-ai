Driver Retention Predictor

AI monitors 50+ behavioral signals to predict which drivers will quit in the next 30-60 days.

What this shows: Each resignation costs $5K-$15K. AI detects patterns (fewer route acceptances, declining safety scores, reduced coaching engagement) that predict turnover weeks before it happens. Click a high-risk driver to see recommended retention actions.
