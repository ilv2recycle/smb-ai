🚀 Your First Practice Session
1
Tune your ukulele. Use a free tuner app — strings should sound G, C, E, A.
2
Learn the C chord first — it only needs one finger! Strum slowly until each string rings clear.
3
Add the F chord. Two fingers. Practice switching C → F → C → F.
4
Strum down only, four times per chord. Keep a steady beat — even slow is fine.
5
Sing along with He Yama Yo! Don't worry about perfection — have fun. 🌺
